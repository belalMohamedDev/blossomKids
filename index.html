Frequently Asked Questions

What is Blossom, and how does it work?

Blossom offers a complete, child-safe digital experience for young Arab learners.

Is Blossom suitable for all age groups?

Yes, Blossom is designed for children aged 2 to 14, with content tailored to different age groups and developmental stages.

How does Blossom ensure my child's safety while using the app?

Blossom implements multiple safety measures including content filtering, parental controls, and a secure environment designed specifically for children.

Can parents track their child's progress on Blossom?

Yes, parents have access to a comprehensive dashboard where they can track learning progress, view reports, and manage their child's experience.

Are there any costs associated with using Blossom?

Blossom offers a free basic plan with limited features, and premium subscription plans for full access to all content and features.
